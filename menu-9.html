LASAGNA

Sushi
$12

Lasagna is a traditional Italian dish that is made of layered pasta, meat or vegetables, and cheese, typically ricotta and mozzarella, and covered with a tomato or meat-based sauce. Lasagna is typically baked in the oven, the pasta sheets are cooked first, then they are layered with the meat or vegetables and cheese mixture, and then covered with the sauce, the process is repeated until all the ingredients are used. This dish can be made with different types of pasta like lasagna noodles, egg noodles, and even zucchini slices to make a low-carb version. The dish is often served as a main course and is a popular comfort food.

Ingredients:

- 1 pound of lasagna noodles

- 1 pound of ground beef or Italian sausage

- 1 onion, diced

- 2 cloves of minced garlic

- 1 can of crushed tomatoes (28 oz)

- 1 cup of water

- 2 tablespoons of tomato paste

- 1 teaspoon of dried basil

- 1 teaspoon of dried oregano

- Salt and pepper, to taste

- 15 oz of ricotta cheese

- 2 cups of shredded mozzarella cheese

- 1/4 cup of grated Parmesan cheese

- 1 egg

- Fresh basil leaves (optional)

Instructions:

1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).

2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.

3. Add the onion and garlic to the skillet and cook until softened.

4. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, water, tomato paste, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper. Bring the mixture to a simmer and cook for about 15 minutes.

5. In a separate bowl, mix together the ricotta cheese, 1 cup of the mozzarella cheese, and the egg.

6. Spread a thin layer of the meat sauce on the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish.

7. Layer the cooked lasagna noodles over the sauce.

8. Spread a layer of the ricotta cheese mixture over the noodles.

9. Repeat the layers of sauce, noodles, and cheese mixture until all the ingredients are used, ending with a layer of meat sauce on top.

10. Sprinkle the remaining mozzarella cheese and grated Parmesan cheese on top of the lasagna.

11. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake for 25 minutes.

12. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes or until the cheese is golden brown and bubbly.

13. Let the lasagna cool for 10 minutes before serving.

14. Garnish with fresh basil leaves, if desired.
