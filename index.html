A burger is a sandwich consisting of one or more cooked patties of ground meat, usually beef, placed inside a sliced bun. Other ingredients such as cheese, lettuce, tomato, and condiments may also be added. Burgers are often served as fast food and are a popular food item in many countries.

Beef Steak
$15

A beef steak is a cut of meat, usually from the beef cattle, that is cut perpendicular to the muscle fibers. The most common cuts of beef steak are ribeye, sirloin, T-bone, and filet mignon. Beef steaks are often grilled, pan-fried, or broiled and are typically served as a main course. They can be cooked to various degrees of doneness, such as rare, medium, or well-done, depending on personal preference.

Hotdog
$8

A hot dog is a type of sausage that is typically made from beef, pork, or a combination of the two. It is usually grilled, steamed, or boiled, and then served in a sliced bun. Hot dogs are often garnished with condiments such as mustard, ketchup, mayonnaise, cheese, onions, and sauerkraut. They are a popular food item in many countries and are often sold as street food or at sporting events and fairs.

ASIAN

Sushi
$12

Sushi is a traditional Japanese dish that typically consists of cooked vinegared rice (sushi-meshi) combined with various ingredients such as seafood, vegetables, and sometimes tropical fruits. The most common seafood used in sushi are raw fish (such as salmon, tuna, and yellowtail) but also seafood like octopus, squid and eel are used. Sushi can be presented in many forms, such as nigiri-zushi (hand-formed sushi), maki-zushi (rolled sushi), and temaki (hand-rolled sushi). Sushi is often served with soy sauce, wasabi, and pickled ginger. Sushi is a popular food worldwide, and many variations and fusion styles have been developed to cater to different cultures and preferences.

Katsu Curry
$15

Katsu curry is a popular dish in Japanese cuisine, which consists of a meat or seafood cutlet that is breaded and deep-fried, and is served with a thick curry sauce. The cutlet is typically made from pork (tonkatsu) or chicken (chicken katsu) and the breading is made from flour, egg, and bread crumbs. The curry sauce is typically made with a roux base and various spices, such as turmeric, cumin, and coriander. The dish is usually served with rice, vegetables, and a salad. Katsu curry is considered to be a Japanese adaptation of the dish "cutlet curry" which was introduced to Japan in the late 19th century by the British.

Ramen
$10

Ramen is a type of Japanese noodle dish that typically consists of Chinese-style wheat noodles served in a meat or fish-based broth, often flavored with soy sauce or miso, and topped with ingredients such as sliced pork, dried seaweed, and green onions. Ramen is considered a popular and iconic dish in Japanese cuisine, and has also gained popularity worldwide in recent years.

EUROPEAN

Spaghetti
$12

Spaghetti is a long, thin, cylindrical pasta of Italian origin. It is made from durum wheat semolina and water. Spaghetti is typically served with a tomato-based sauce, but can also be served with other sauces such as meat sauce, olive oil and garlic, or seafood. Spaghetti is one of the most well-known and popular types of pasta in the world and is a staple dish in Italian-American cuisine.

Pizza
$20

Pizza is a traditional Italian dish that is made of a round, flattened dough base, typically made from wheat flour, yeast, water, and salt, that is topped with various ingredients such as tomato sauce, cheese, meats, vegetables, and herbs. The dough is usually stretched by hand or rolled with a rolling pin, then topped and baked in an oven at a high temperature, traditionally in a wood-fired oven. Pizza is one of the most popular foods in the world, and it comes in many variations such as Neapolitan, New York-style, Chicago-style, and more. The toppings can vary depending on the region, and it can be made with different types of cheese like mozzarella, provolone, gorgonzola, etc. Pizza can be served hot or cold, it can be made in different shapes and sizes. Pizza is often served as a main course and it's a popular comfort food.

Lasagna
$10

Lasagna is a traditional Italian dish that is made of layered pasta, meat or vegetables, and cheese, typically ricotta and mozzarella, and covered with a tomato or meat-based sauce. Lasagna is typically baked in the oven, the pasta sheets are cooked first, then they are layered with the meat or vegetables and cheese mixture, and then covered with the sauce, the process is repeated until all the ingredients are used. This dish can be made with different types of pasta like lasagna noodles, egg noodles, and even zucchini slices to make a low-carb version. The dish is often served as a main course and is a popular comfort food.
