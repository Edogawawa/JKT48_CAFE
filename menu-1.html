CHEESEBURGER

Cheeseburger
$8

A burger is a sandwich consisting of one or more cooked patties of ground meat, usually beef, placed inside a sliced bun. Other ingredients such as cheese, lettuce, tomato, and condiments may also be added. Burgers are often served as fast food and are a popular food item in many countries.

Ingredients:

- 1 pound ground beef (80/20 or 85/15)

- Salt and pepper

- 4 slices of cheese (cheddar, American, or your choice)

- 4 hamburger buns

- Optional toppings (lettuce, tomato, onion, pickles, mustard, ketchup, mayonnaise, etc.)

Instructions:

1. Divide the ground beef into 4 equal portions and shape them into patties. Season both sides of the patties with salt and pepper.

2. Heat a skillet or grill over medium-high heat. Place the patties on the skillet or grill and cook for about 4 minutes per side for a medium-rare burger, or longer if you prefer your burger more well-done.

3. During the last minute of cooking, add a slice of cheese on top of each patty.

4. Toast the hamburger buns if desired.

5. Assemble the burgers by placing a patty on the bottom half of each bun, then add your desired toppings, and top with the other half of the bun.

6. Serve and enjoy!
