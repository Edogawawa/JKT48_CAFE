HOTDOG

Hotdog
$8

A hot dog is a type of sausage that is typically made from beef, pork, or a combination of the two. It is usually grilled, steamed, or boiled, and then served in a sliced bun. Hot dogs are often garnished with condiments such as mustard, ketchup, mayonnaise, cheese, onions, and sauerkraut. They are a popular food item in many countries and are often sold as street food or at sporting events and fairs.

Ingredients:

- Hotdog buns

- Hotdogs (beef, pork, turkey, or vegetarian)

- Optional toppings (mustard, ketchup, mayonnaise, cheese, lettuce, tomato, onion, relish, sauerkraut, etc.)

Instructions:

1. Heat a skillet or grill to medium-high heat.

2. Place the hotdogs on the skillet or grill and cook them for about 3-5 minutes, or until they are heated through and have a slight char on the outside.

3. Toast the hotdog buns if desired

4. Assemble the hotdogs by placing a hotdog in the bun, then add your desired toppings.

5. Serve and enjoy!
