PIZZA

Pizza
$20

Pizza is a traditional Italian dish that is made of a round, flattened dough base, typically made from wheat flour, yeast, water, and salt, that is topped with various ingredients such as tomato sauce, cheese, meats, vegetables, and herbs. The dough is usually stretched by hand or rolled with a rolling pin, then topped and baked in an oven at a high temperature, traditionally in a wood-fired oven. Pizza is one of the most popular foods in the world, and it comes in many variations such as Neapolitan, New York-style, Chicago-style, and more. The toppings can vary depending on the region, and it can be made with different types of cheese like mozzarella, provolone, gorgonzola, etc. Pizza can be served hot or cold, it can be made in different shapes and sizes. Pizza is often served as a main course and it's a popular comfort food.

Ingredients:

- 3 cups of all-purpose flour

- 1 envelope of active dry yeast

- 1 teaspoon of sugar

- 1 teaspoon of salt

- 1 cup of warm water

- 2 tablespoons of olive oil

Instructions:

1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, yeast, sugar, and salt.

2. In a separate small bowl, mix together the warm water and olive oil.

3. Slowly pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and mix with a spoon or a stand mixer with a dough hook attachment.

4. Once the dough comes together, knead it by hand on a floured surface for about 5-7 minutes or until it becomes smooth and elastic.

5. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover it with a clean towel or plastic wrap, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1 hour or until doubled in size.

6. Once the dough has risen, punch it down to release the air, and divide it into 2 or 3 equal portions.

7. Roll out the dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness, usually around 1/4 inch.

8. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C)

9. Place the dough on a baking sheet or pizza pan.

10. Add your desired toppings, such as tomato sauce, cheese, and any additional toppings.

11. Bake in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly.

12. Let it cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.
