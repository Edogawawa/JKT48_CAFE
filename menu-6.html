RAMEN

Ramen
$10

Ramen is a type of Japanese noodle dish that typically consists of Chinese-style wheat noodles served in a meat or fish-based broth, often flavored with soy sauce or miso, and topped with ingredients such as sliced pork, dried seaweed, and green onions. Ramen is considered a popular and iconic dish in Japanese cuisine, and has also gained popularity worldwide in recent years.

Ingredients:

- 4 packs of instant ramen noodles (without seasoning packets)

- 4 cups of chicken or vegetable broth

- 2 cloves of minced garlic

- 1 tablespoon of grated ginger

- 2 tablespoons of soy sauce

- 2 tablespoons of miso paste

- 1 teaspoon of sesame oil

- 2 cups of sliced mushrooms

- 2 cups of sliced bok choy or other greens

- 2 eggs (optional)

- Green onions or cilantro for garnish

Instructions:

1. Bring a pot of water to a boil and cook the ramen noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.

2. In a separate pot, bring the broth to a simmer and add the garlic and ginger. Cook for a few minutes until fragrant.

3. Stir in the soy sauce, miso paste, and sesame oil. Add the mushrooms and bok choy and cook until tender.

4. In a separate pan, fry or boil the eggs to your liking.

5. Divide the cooked ramen noodles into four bowls.

6. Ladle the broth and vegetables over the noodles.

7. Slice the eggs and place on top of the ramen.

8. Garnish with green onions or cilantro and serve.
