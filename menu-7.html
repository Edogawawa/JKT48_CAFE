SPAGHETTI

Spaghetti
$12

Spaghetti is a long, thin, cylindrical pasta of Italian origin. It is made from durum wheat semolina and water. Spaghetti is typically served with a tomato-based sauce, but can also be served with other sauces such as meat sauce, olive oil and garlic, or seafood. Spaghetti is one of the most well-known and popular types of pasta in the world and is a staple dish in Italian-American cuisine.

Ingredients:

- 1 pound of spaghetti

- 1/4 cup of olive oil

- 4 cloves of minced garlic

- 1 can of crushed tomatoes (28 oz)

- Salt and pepper, to taste

- Fresh basil leaves (optional)

- Parmesan cheese (optional)

Instructions:

1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the spaghetti and cook according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.

2. In a separate pan, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the garlic and cook until fragrant, about 1 minute.

3. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, salt, and pepper. Bring the mixture to a simmer and cook for about 10 minutes.

4. Toss the cooked spaghetti with the tomato sauce.

5. Serve with fresh basil leaves and grated Parmesan cheese, if desired.
